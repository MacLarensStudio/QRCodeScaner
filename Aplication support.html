Simple LogBook App V1.0

Simple LogBook ia a simple an easy to use, but effective IOS application. It designed for pilots all over the world who want to calculate own total flight hours.

Supports most known types: QR Code, Barcode, Code39, Code93, Code128, EAN-8, EAN-13, Aztec, PDF417, ITF-14, DataMatrix ...Easy scan QR Code and Barcode

Futures: - Quick recognition - Flash suport - Scan history - Sharing - No AD - Unlimited Scans - Jump directly to WEB addresses decoded
